The Best Movies According To Mr.Probash

My top 5 goat movies.

Interstellar

"Interstellar" is a mind-bending sci-fi epic exploring love, survival, and the mysteries of space-time through a father-daughter bond.

Inception

"Inception" is a thrilling exploration of dreams within dreams, where a skilled thief navigates layered realities to plant an idea in someone's mind.

Oppenheimer

"Oppenheimer" is a gripping biographical drama that delves into the life of J. Robert Oppenheimer and the moral complexities of creating the atomic bomb.

Godfather

"The Godfather" is a timeless masterpiece chronicling the rise and fall of a powerful mafia family, blending loyalty, power, and betrayal.

The dark knight rises

"The Dark Knight Rises" is an epic conclusion to the trilogy, where Batman faces his ultimate challenge to save Gotham from the vengeful Bane and rediscover hope.
